Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is one of Pakistan's signature and most famous dish. In simple terms you can call it spicy rice but this description doesn't do it justice. Biryani is made with a wide variety of sub-continental spices and herbs.

Let's see what ingredients you need to make Biryani

Biryani Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Shan Biryani Masala
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Mint Leaves
  • Water
  • Oil

Now with the ingredients out of the way let's get to the steps you'll have to follow to make Biryani

Biryani making steps

  1. Boil 2 glass water in pressure cooker
  2. Add 1 glass Rice in boiling water and close pressure cooker
  3. Put Pressure Cooker on stove till it is fully pressurized
  4. Stir fry chicken on medium heat
  5. Add yogurt and shan masala in the chicken
  6. Add 1 glass water in chicken after cooking for 5 minutes
  7. Close lid and wait till chicken is cooked
  8. Mix cooked rice curry and rice together

Voila you Biryani is made!!1
